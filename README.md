# Superstore Regional Sales Performance Analysis

This repository contains an interactive Business Intelligence (BI) dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI, alongside the complete final analysis report and presentation slides[cite: 1]. The project focuses on transforming raw transactional retail data (2020–2023) into actionable business insights[cite: 1].

## 📊 Project Overview
* **Objective:** To monitor and evaluate regional sales performance across the United States, tracking Year-over-Year (YoY) growth trends for Total Revenue, Total Profit, and Total Quantity[cite: 1].
* **Architecture:** Implements a clean Star Schema data model with a dedicated Date Table to drive dynamic DAX time-intelligence metrics and visual KPI indicators[cite: 1].
* **Core Metrics Tracked:** Revenue, Net Profit, Product Margins, and seasonal purchasing spikes[cite: 1].

## 💡 Key Analytical Findings
* **Central Region (The Margin Squeeze):** Generated the highest total revenue (\$891K) and product volume (12,884 units), but profit growth trailed volume growth due to margin pressures[cite: 1].
* **West Region (Consistent Scaling):** Exhibited the most stable upward trajectory, nearly doubling its starting performance over the 4-year cycle without experiencing a down year[cite: 1].
* **South Region (Segment Vulnerability):** Remains the smallest structural market and is heavily dependent on Corporate clients, creating exposure if corporate demand shifts[cite: 1].
* **East Region (Balanced Recovery):** Rebounded sharply after a 2021 decline, achieving a well-diversified customer foundation highlighted by strong growth in the Small Business category[cite: 1].

## 🌍 Social Impact & SDG Alignment
This project actively demonstrates the integration of technology into modern management practices, aligning with global indicators for development:
* **Primary SDG 8 (Decent Work & Economic Growth):** Upgrades standard business infrastructure to interactive BI, directly supporting higher economic productivity through technological upgrading (Target 8.2)[cite: 1].
* **Secondary SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption & Production):** Maps year-end demand surges (Q4 spikes) to help businesses optimize inventory management, preventing overstocking and physical retail waste (Target 12.5)[cite: 1].
